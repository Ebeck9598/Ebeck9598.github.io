Justin James Watt

J.J watt is more then just a football player; he is someone who we can all look up to. He embodies a lot of great qualities that everyone strives for.

Some of these key qualites include:

  • Motivating others- J.J Watt has motivated people to donate money after hurricane Harvey and has even created his own foundation to help motivate kids.
  • Determination– strives to be the best he can be on and off the field. He always prepares himself for football with crazy workout routines and diets. Off the field he is always doing what he can to help people in need./li>
  • Big Voice- Always brings up difficult topics and uses his platform to talk about them and bring awareness.
  • Commitment- Always committed to be the overall best person he can be and committed to being a humanitarian.

J.J Watt is an incredibly successful football player that uses his money and platform to help people. He is what every athlete should aspire to be and has qualities everyone should value. J.J Watt has continuously helped people who are in need and does his best to inspire change when it is needed.
